## Learner / didactic intent (from Media)

- **unit type:** procedure / decision (framework, not emergency card)
- **learner job:** execute R first → S → I → name C → safety-net; never skip to “treat”
- **load risk:** table equalizes steps; binary “kein C ohne R+I+S” invisible; looks like emergency algorithm wrongly
- **presentation hypothesis:** numbered operational sequence with step weight + binary rule loud — **method OS**, not 112-banner chrome
- **why graphic:** order + asymmetry of defaults must be *seen* (R/S default danger, I burden of proof) without becoming a notfall-karte

## Content to show (claim-safe — from pilot)

| step | allowed text (German) |
|---|---|
| 1 R zuerst | Wenn Lebensgefahr-Cluster / schwere RF → *C: Stop & 112 / ED* — I und „Nutzen“ nachrangig |
| 2 S klären | Wenn geplante Maßnahme unsicher → Maßnahme stoppen/ändern (*S4/S3*); ggf. nur PT-Lite (*S2*) |
