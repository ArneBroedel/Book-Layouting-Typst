### Interpretation (E8)

The legacy pilot proves **layout dogfood** of bookkit against the same content file, but skips the publishing roles workflow. The compose pilot trades raw form *count* (7 vs 6) for **intent**: fewer repeated tables, dedicated `ddx-box`, early single gatekeeper, claims demoted to audit appendix, and an explicit **named gap** instead of silent bullet substitution. Process is the larger delta: converter-only vs. gated Media‚ÜíTech‚ÜíValidate chain required by CONSENSUS / E7.
